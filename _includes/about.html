When is this event happening?

  • Nov 2022 - Feb 2023

    Expert lectures

    A number of industry experts have been invited to share their story and answer your questions about game making.

    Do you want to form your own company? Work for a AAA studio? Research games at university?

    They can answer your questions!

  • 20th Feb 2023 - 24th Feb 2023

    Workshop week

    We will be running online workshops to introduce a number of different tools that will help you make your games. The sessions will cover free tools for art work, sound effects, music making and more...

  • 3rd Mar 2023 - 5th Mar 2023

    Build your game!

    The main event! Work with your team mates to build a game that relates to the secret theme (announced on Friday 3rd March).

    You need to have something playable (and hopefully fun!) submitted by 18:00 CST on Sunday 5th March.

  • 6th Mar 2023 - 10th Mar 2023

    Judging process

    Our expert panel of judges will review the submissions and decide on the winners for each age bracket.
