3rd March - 5th March 2023

A hackathon for building games

The Dulwich Game Jam is a brand-new game-making competition open to students across all Dulwich schools.

Over the next few weeks, we have invited some amazing guest speakers to share their expertise and experiences in working within the games industry. Additionally, in collaboration with Ubisoft - one of the world's largest gaming companies - we are providing students who sign up with an online game-making workshop on the 16th of February. Moreover, participating students will also have access to a direct line of communication with Ubisoft staff for 2 weeks before the event so you can take any questions straight to the experts.

For the game jam weekend itself, work with up to 7 friends to design and create a card game, board game or video game related to a secret theme. Once submitted, your games will be judged by experts from the games industry. There are some amazing prizes up for grabs for teams who make the best games.
